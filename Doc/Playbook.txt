

# Mindbrain Enterprise Compliance Playbook

## 1. Introduction

This compliance playbook defines the mandatory legal, operational, data security, financial, and vendor governance standards that all third-party contracts, vendor agreements, NDAs, SaaS agreements, and employment-related documents must comply with before approval.

Any deviation from these standards must be flagged during AI audit review.

---

## 2. Payment Terms & Financial Policies

* Payment terms must never exceed NET-30.
* Advance payment clauses are preferred for high-value engagements.
* Late payment penalties above 5% are prohibited.
* Hidden processing fees are not allowed.
* Currency conversion liability must not fall entirely on Mindbrain.
* Automatic price increase clauses require prior written approval.
* Vendors cannot suspend critical services without a minimum 30-day notice.
* Refund obligations must exist for failed deliverables.

---

## 3. Liability & Indemnification Rules

* Total liability must not exceed $100,000.
* Unlimited liability clauses are strictly prohibited.
* Consequential damages must always be excluded.
* Indirect damages must be excluded.
* Vendors must indemnify Mindbrain against third-party IP infringement claims.
* Liability for cybersecurity negligence must remain with the vendor.
* Liability caps cannot be removed without executive approval.

---

## 4. Confidentiality & Information Protection

* Every agreement must contain a confidentiality clause.
* Confidentiality obligations must survive termination for at least 3 years.
* Vendors cannot disclose customer data to subcontractors without approval.
* Internal business strategies must be classified as confidential information.
* AI training on uploaded company data is prohibited.
* Confidential data storage must use encryption standards.

---

## 5. Data Protection & Privacy Compliance

* All vendors must comply with GDPR-equivalent standards.
* Customer data cannot be sold or monetized.
* Data breaches must be reported within 48 hours.
* Vendors must provide audit logs upon request.
* User data retention beyond contract termination requires written approval.
* Cross-border data transfers require legal review.
* Data processors must maintain encryption at rest and in transit.
* Sensitive customer data cannot be shared with advertising networks.

---

## 6. Termination & Exit Clauses

* Minimum termination notice period must be 30 days.
* Immediate unilateral termination rights are prohibited.
* Mindbrain must retain data export rights after termination.
* Vendors must support transition assistance for at least 15 days after contract termination.
* Early termination penalties above 10% contract value are prohibited.

---

## 7. Arbitration & Governing Law

* Arbitration clauses are mandatory.
* Governing law must be India.
* Jurisdiction must be Bhubaneswar, Odisha.
* International dispute forums require executive approval.
* Litigation-first agreements are discouraged.

---

## 8. Intellectual Property Ownership

* All deliverables created for Mindbrain remain property of Mindbrain.
* Vendors cannot reuse proprietary code, APIs, prompts, or internal workflows.
* AI-generated outputs created for Mindbrain belong exclusively to Mindbrain.
* Open-source dependencies must be disclosed.
* Source code escrow clauses are recommended for enterprise vendors.

---

## 9. Security Requirements

* Vendors must maintain industry-standard encryption.
* Access logs must be retained for 90 days.
* MFA must be enabled for privileged accounts.
* Penetration testing reports may be requested annually.
* Security incidents must be disclosed immediately.
* Vendors handling financial or legal records must maintain secure backups.

---

## 10. Non-Compete & Restrictive Covenants

* Non-compete duration must never exceed 12 months.
* Global non-compete restrictions are prohibited.
* Restrictions must be geographically reasonable.
* Employee mobility rights must not be excessively restricted.

---

## 11. Auto-Renewal & Subscription Governance

* Auto-renewal requires 30-day prior written notice.
* Hidden renewal penalties are prohibited.
* Subscription lock-in beyond 12 months requires approval.
* Vendors must provide cancellation workflows.

---

## 12. Vendor Operational Requirements

* SLAs must clearly define uptime commitments.
* Critical vendors must provide escalation contacts.
* Subcontractor usage requires disclosure.
* Service interruptions must be communicated proactively.

---

## 13. AI & Technology Governance

* Company legal documents cannot be used to train public AI models.
* AI vendors must disclose data retention policies.
* AI-generated legal outputs require human review.
* Sensitive legal documents must remain isolated from public datasets.

---

## 14. Compliance Enforcement

Any clause violating this playbook must be:

* flagged during AI analysis
* categorized by severity
* explained with legal implications
* provided with safer counter-clause recommendations
